How Our Marketplace Works

For Users:

  • Browse & Buy: Find your favorite products, add them to the cart, and place your order.
  • Track Your Order: Receive real-time updates on your delivery status.
  • Receive & Rate: Get your items delivered to your door and leave feedback.

For Vendors:

  • Sign Up: Create an account and set up your business profile.
  • List Your Products: Add product details, pricing, and inventory.
  • Manage Orders: Receive, prepare, and track customer orders from your dashboard.

Dispatchers/Riders:

  • Join the Network: Sign up, provide vehicle details, and start receiving delivery requests.
  • Pick & Deliver: Collect orders from vendors and deliver them to users.
  • Earn & Rate: Get paid for deliveries and maintain a high rating for better opportunities.
